Ocean freight- Containerized, Break-bulk and oversized cargo.

Transportation- Domestic trucking

Local Customs clearance- As clearing agents, Collossians international undertakes clearing and forwarding of all consignments and do not rely on third parties. clearance of all your goods are handled professionally either via air or sea (LCL, FCL, Break-bulk, Vehicles)

  • Customs Brokerage-
    • Processing of import documents (Form 'M', Insurance certificate, PAAR, SONCAP),
    • Collecting documents (waybill or bill of lading) on behalf of importer.
    • Making arrangements for customs bill of entry (printout and assessment).
    • Payment of custom duty and other related charges to the government through the bank which is based on Assessment and printout.
    • ositioning of shipment for physical or scan examination and release by Nigeria Customs.
    • Processing of delivery order from shipping line thus obtaining authority to load consignment after payment of shipping charges.
    • Arrangement to transfer to any bonded terminals.
    • Arrangement to load shipment on truck and deliver to designated warehouse or any location given.
